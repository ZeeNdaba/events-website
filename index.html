About Us

Hello, we are Sharon and Vimbai - the creative duo behind unforgettable celebrations in Bonnie Scotland. We have a passion for beautifully curated gatherings that reflect personality and purpose. We bring a fresh and artistic approach to every event we touch. With a serious eye for detail, a flair for design, and a heart full of passion, we plan and style events that feel as good as they look.


What sets us apart? We are not just here to make things pretty - we're here to make them personal. At the heart of everything we do is our obsession with the little things, our love for building genuine relationships, and our commitment to creating one-of-a-kind gatherings that feel like you. No Pinterest copy-and-paste - just original, tailored designs that tell your story. Whether it's a dreamy wedding, a milestone celebration, or a just-because gathering, we are ready to bring your vision alive with a whole of sparkle. Let's create something unforgettable together!
