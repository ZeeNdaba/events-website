Our Services

We specialise in creating thoughtfully planned and beautifully styled events that reflect your unique story. Whether you are starting with a blank canvas or need support bringing your vision to life, we are here to make the process smooth, enjoyable, and truly memorable.

Event Planning

From concept to completion, we manage every detail. This includes timelines, logistics, supplier coordination and more – so you can focus on enjoying the moment, stress-free.

Event Design & Styling

We design visually cohesive events that feel personal and intentional. From colour palettes to tablescapes, we craft spaces that reflect your style — not someone else's Pinterest board. We create bespoke mood boards that help you visualise your special day.

Décor & Set-Up

Whether you're after something elegant and understated or bold and creative, we bring your event vision to life with carefully curated décor and seamless on-the-day styling. This service includes setting up and take down service after your event.

On-the-day Coordination

If you have done the planning but need someone to take the reins on the day, we ensure everything runs smoothly – managing the timeline, suppliers, and last-minute details.

Bespoke Packages

No two events are the same, and neither are our services. We offer flexible, tailored packages designed to suit your needs, preferences, and budget.

Our Process

STEP 1 Questions

To help provide a quotation, we will ask you to answer some questions based on your event.

STEP 2 Quotation

We will then provide a quotation in 3–7 working days. If you are happy to proceed, we will ask you to send us a confirmation email.

STEP 3 Finalisation

If you are happy to proceed, we will ask you to send us a confirmation email.

STEP 4 Deposit

A deposit secures your booking and allows us to begin planning in detail.

STEP 5 Planning

The fun part begins! We will have regular meetings with you and the venue to ensure everything goes smoothly.

STEP 6 Event Day

Relax and enjoy your special day while we manage the details to make unforgettable memories.
